    Game OverView

    Tennis is a racket sport that can be played individually against a single opponent (singles) or between two teams of two players each (doubles). The game is played on a rectangular court divided by a net, with players using a stringed racket to hit a hollow rubber ball covered with felt over or around the net and into the opponent's court.
    The objective of tennis is to score points by hitting the ball in such a way that the opponent is not able to play a valid return. The game can be played on various surfaces such as grass, clay, and hard courts, each adding a unique twist to the gameplay.

    Game HighLights

    Four Grand Slam Tournaments: The pinnacle of tennis competition includes the Australian Open, French Open, Wimbledon, and the US Open. Each tournament brings its own unique flair and challenges, attracting the best players from around the globe

    Iconic Rivalries: Tennis has seen some of the most thrilling rivalries in sports history. From Federer vs. Nadal to Williams vs. Sharapova, these matches have delivered unforgettable moments.

    Legendary Players: Icons like Roger Federer, Serena Williams, Rafael Nadal, and Novak Djokovic have not only dominated the sport but have also inspired new generations of players and fans.

    Olympic Sport: Tennis has been a part of the modern Olympic Games since 1988, with players competing for the prestigious gold medal in singles and doubles events.

    Global Appeal: Tennis is played and watched by millions across the world, making it one of the most popular and inclusive sports.
